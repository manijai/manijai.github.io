Thank You!

The number of memorable days in a year are few and far between. August 15th, for instance, when we remember those patriots who sacrificed so much for the independence that we enjoy today. Diwali, the festival of lights, but then, we are never sure when this day of dazzling celebrations occurs. The other days are usually... just that - other days. Merely meant to be passed by; not savoured, relished and looked forward to.
Your entry into my life has added meaning to one of these meaningless dates. More than that, it has made this a memorable day for me to look forward to.
Thank you for making the 10th of December the most Memorable Day on my calendar!

I Hate You!

I used to look forward to the 01st of January - New Year's Day. I used to look forward to Diwali. I used to look forward to... okay, you get the idea, right?
These were occasions when I could be with family and friends, enjoy the festivities and generally let my hair down. But, alas! You've made me celebrate New Year's Day 21 days soon. You've made me celebrate Diwali 21 days late.
What would the world think seeing me cut a New Year on December 10? How would people react seeing me burst Diwali crackers on the 10th of December? Do you know how much I hate you for turning my world topsy-turvy?
But, I will let you in on a secret - I couldn't care less what people or the world think when I celebrate New Year or Diwali on December 10. For me, December 10th is going to be my New Year's Day. December 10th is going to be my Diwali. December 10th is going to be my day of bright sunshine, of beautiful and sweet smelling flowers, of delightful sea breezes, of joy and of happiness.
Welcome the 10th of December.
